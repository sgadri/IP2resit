Earthquakes

The earthquakes are a result of the shaking of surface of the earth from a sudden release of energy in the Earth's lithosphere. The earthquakes are very common in certain places around the world and their instensity is usually low so they don't threaten people's lives. But these earthquakes should be followed and monitored all the time. This data set shows some of latest earthquakes by storing the coordinates of the locations where earthquakes took place.

Covid-19

Covid 19 pandemic known as the coronavirus is an ongoing pandemic that took the world by surprise. It has infected more than 85.7 Million people and killed over 1.8 Million. These numbers are rising daily and this data set is following this rise of the virus and updating everyday. The chart in the covid 19 page of this website shows the daily rising counts during a 7 day period of time. It shows the number of the infected, deceased, and recovered in different countries around the globe. There are some missing numbers in some countries, these missing values will be displayed as a 0.
